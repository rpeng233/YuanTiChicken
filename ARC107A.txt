We need to compute

$$
\sum_{a=1}^{A}\sum_{b=1}^{B}\sum_{c=1}^{C} abc
\pmod{998244353}.
$$

Since the variables are independent, the sum factors as

$$
\left(\sum_{a=1}^{A}a\right)
\left(\sum_{b=1}^{B}b\right)
\left(\sum_{c=1}^{C}c\right).
$$

Using

$$
1+2+\cdots+n=\frac{n(n+1)}{2},
$$

the answer is

$$
\frac{A(A+1)}{2}
\cdot
\frac{B(B+1)}{2}
\cdot
\frac{C(C+1)}{2}
\pmod{998244353}.
$$

Compute each triangular number modulo (998244353), then multiply the three results modulo (998244353).
